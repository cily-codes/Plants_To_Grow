    Four Mistakes People Make
    With Indoor Plants
  • Over-watering
    Ironically, the most fequent casue of houseplants not surviving is Over-watering.

  • Too Much SunlightS
    Some people think all plants need a lot of lights. Light is essential, but as always with plants, more is often too much.

  • Thinking All Plants Need A Lot Of Maintenance
    So many people believe all plants require an awful amount of maintenance and care, which is not in any way true.

  • Moving Plants All The Time
    Moving your plant all the time isn't healthy for the plant. Stability is essential for your plants to adapt to its new habitat.




    RECOMMENDED WAYS TO CARE FOR YOUR PLANTS

  • On average, plants are watered once a week. It is best to check the top soil by dipping your finger and water when top inch is dry to the touch.

  • When watering, make sure to water all the way through the pot. Watering around the plant is best to allow balanced mositure throughout the soil mix.

  • Dust may build up on leaves. To bring bring back the shine of the leaves, you can wipe them with damp cloth or if you can, give them a shower during watering day.

  • Plants should have enough sunlight. Plants along the dark corners of the house should be rotated to areas near windows so that it can absorb indirect sunlight.
